Access & Contact

MPSD is a semi-public beta resource. Access requests are welcome from academic researchers and central banks.

How to Request Access

In addition to academic research users, we also offer access to central banks interested in using MPSD for research, policy analysis, and institutional work.

  1. 1
    Complete the Sign-up Form

    Fill out the Official Registration Form to provide your name, affiliation, and research intent.

  2. 2
    Provide your GitHub Username

    Ensure your GitHub handle is included correctly in the form.

  3. 3
    Wait for Authorization

    Once your request is processed, your GitHub account will be granted access to this repository. Users may be contacted regarding updates and related research outputs.
