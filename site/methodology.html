Data Construction

How MPSD is built

A transparent, reproducible pipeline for central bank statement collection, normalization, and analysis-ready outputs.

Coverage

Policy statements from 51 central banks, spanning 1990-2024, with standardized metadata and publication timestamps.

Pipeline

Combines document parsing, NLP preprocessing, and LLM-assisted aspect-level sentiment and question-answer extraction.

Outputs

Structured files designed for panel data analysis, event studies, forecasting, and cross-country communication metrics.

Reproducibility

Versioned dataset releases and accompanying code support verification and extension of published research findings.
